# Refund and Cancellation Policy
_Last update 25th June 2026_  
We hope you are able to make your appointment with us. However, if for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchase, or you are otherwise unable to attend, you may contact us to request a full refund or an exchange. Please see below for more information on our refund and cancellation policy.

## CANCELLATIONS
All cancellations must be requested no less than twenty-four (24) hours before your appointment start time.

## CANCELLATION PROCESS
To request a cancellation, please contact us using our contact form at [procabsim.com/contact-us](https://procabsim.com/contact-us)

## REFUNDS
After confirming your cancellation, we will process your refund. We will contact you by email when your cancellation has been received. Please allow at least seven (7) days from receipt of confirmation of your cancellation to process your refund.

## EXCEPTIONS
If you are unhappy with your experience with us, please contact us at the contact details below to arrange a refund.  
Please note, refunds are not guaranteed to be authorised, this will be decided on a case by case basis depending on the circumstances and reason for the request. We reserve the right to deny a refund.

## QUESTIONS
If you have any questions concerning our refund and cancellation policy, please contact us using our contact form at [procabsim.com/contact-us](https://procabsim.com/contact-us)
